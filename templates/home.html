Boston Housing Price Prediction Model





About the data

The model predicts the housing prices in Boston area. The dataset used contains information collected by the U.S Census Service concerning housing in the area of Boston Mass.

Since the dataset is quite old, the housing prices predicted does not reflect the current prices by any means.

Though the original dataset can be obtained from the StatLib archive, in this project, I have used the pre-loaded dataset from Scikit learn library.
